About Me

High School

  I am an aspiring Software engineer. My journey to becoming a Software engineer started long before I even realized it. As a kid, I enjoyed solving puzzles and problems. In middle school, I enjoyed math more than any other subject and I looked forward to it every day. Before going to high school, I knew I wanted to pursue something that had to do with problem solving and math. My parents suggested that I should become an engineer. I was eager to learn and wanted to start right away. For me to do that, I need to go to a high school that had an engineering program. I chose to apply to a technical school called Passaic County Technical Institute and I applied to be in their engineering program. I got in.

 There my freshman engineering teacher started to challenge my perspective and ways of thinking. It was hard but I enjoyed it. He also exposed my class to logical thinking by using Python. He assigned us varying challenges to solve and I enjoyed them. I fell in love with the challenge of trying to solve a problem by thinking in various ways. This also sparked a passion for learning and reinforce my enjoyment of thinking and problem-solving. Again, every day I looked forward to school and being able to challenge myself. However, I never knew that Computer Science existed or that Software Engineering was a career option. In the next 3 years of my high school career, I was given opportunities to program in RobotC for engineering class projects. In group projects, I would look for any opportunity to code the logic of the project.
